# Disaster-responce-drone
Low-cost disaster response drone designed for aerial surveillance, search and rescue support, and real-time disaster assessment using FPV technology.
## Overview

The Disaster Response Scout Drone is a low-cost unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) developed to assist rescue teams during natural disasters such as floods, earthquakes, cyclones, and landslides. The drone provides real-time aerial surveillance, helping emergency responders assess affected areas, identify safe routes, and support search and rescue operations.

This project focuses on providing rapid situational awareness in locations where ground access is difficult or dangerous.

## Problem Statement

During natural disasters, rescue teams often face challenges in accessing affected areas due to damaged infrastructure, flooding, or debris. Lack of real-time information can delay rescue operations and increase risks for both victims and responders.

This project addresses the problem by providing an aerial platform capable of monitoring disaster zones and transmitting live video to rescue personnel.

## Hardware Components

### Flight System

* SpeedyBee F405 V3 Flight Controller
* F450 Quadcopter Frame
* Brushless Motors
* Electronic Speed Controllers (ESCs)
* Li-Po Battery
* Propellers

### Communication System

* FlySky FS-i6 Transmitter
* FlySky FS-iA6B Receiver

### Surveillance System

* LST S2 FPV Camera
* Skydroid UVC Mini FPV Receiver


## Working Principle

1. The drone is deployed in the disaster zone.
2. The FPV camera captures live aerial footage.
3. Video is transmitted wirelessly to the ground operator.
4. Rescue teams analyze the live feed.
5. Critical areas and possible victim locations are identified.
6. Response teams are guided based on aerial observations.
